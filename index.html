Knowledge Institute of Technology is the brain child of eminent professors from the leading Engineering Colleges, budding entrepreneurs and friends with a vision to build our Nation through quality education.


The Knowledge Institute of Technology (KIOT) in Salem, Tamil Nadu, is an autonomous institution recognized for its dedication to providing quality education in the field of engineering and technology. Established in 2009, KIOT offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including B.E., B.Tech, M.E., and MBA, across various disciplines. As an autonomous institution, KIOT has the flexibility to design and update its curriculum to meet industry standards, ensuring that students are equipped with the latest knowledge and skills.

KIOT also places a strong emphasis on research and development, encouraging both students and faculty to engage in innovative projects. The institution's robust placement cell collaborates with leading companies to provide students with excellent training and placement opportunities, reflecting its commitment to student success. Moreover, KIOT fosters a vibrant student life with a variety of extracurricular activities, technical clubs, and cultural events, all of which contribute to the holistic development of its students. With its strong academic programs, experienced faculty, and industry collaborations, KIOT continues to be a leading choice for engineering education in the region.
